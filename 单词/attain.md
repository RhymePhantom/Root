# Means
- to succeed in getting sth, usually after a lot of effort（通常经过努力）获得，得到
- to reach a particular age, level or condition达到（某年龄、水平、状况）
# Context
The cheetah can attain speeds of up to 97 kph.猎豹的奔跑速度每小时可达97公里。
