# Means
- to make sb feel very ashamed or embarrassed使难堪；使羞愧
# Context
She was mortified to realize he had heard every word she said.她意识到自己的每句话都被他听到了，直羞得无地自容。
How mortifying to have to apologize to him!要向他道歉，多难为情啊！
