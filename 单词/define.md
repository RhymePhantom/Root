# Means
- to say or explain what the meaning of a word or phrase is解释（词语）的含义；给（词语）下定义
- to show clearly a line, shape or edge画出…的线条；描出…的外形；确定…的界线；界定
# Context
The mountain was **sharply defined** against the sky.那座山在天空的衬托下显得轮廓分明。
